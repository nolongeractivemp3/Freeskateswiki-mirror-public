The flip stomp is a variation of the normal stomp dating back at least (probably early https://youtu.be/0-VxiYsQR1o?is=PUiu_9qvF2P6PTlB). To do the flip stomp you start like with the normal stomp but let the skate do a full rotation before landing beneath your feet.

A totorial for the modern flip stomp can be found from jmk: https://youtu.be/6t73GNw4G1g?is=Wnvq4yMxDbyco6QB
