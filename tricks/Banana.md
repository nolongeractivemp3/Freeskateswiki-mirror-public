# Banana

</div>

The Banana is an iconic beginner trick where you toss your skate (typically the back foot) so it rolls ahead in a sweeping arc while you coast on one foot, then step back onto it to complete the catch.

## media
[JMK](/brands/jmk-ride) published a tutorial video on the trick [here](https://youtu.be/gUkGa6rq2Rg?si=_8nz9QuwjXYjudna)
## Tips for learning
- Stationary Toss: Practice throwing the skate in an arc and stepping onto it while standing still to get used to the movement.
- Two-Footed Rolling Arc: Practice tossing the skate in an arc while rolling on two feet before attempting to coast on one foot.
