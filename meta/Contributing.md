# Contributing to the Freeskates Wiki

The Freeskates Wiki is community-driven, and contributions of all sizes are welcome. Whether you want to fix a typo, add new information, or create an entirely new page, there are several ways to help.

## Suggesting Changes

If you don't want to edit the wiki yourself, you can suggest changes through any of the following:

* Leave a comment on the relevant wiki page (requires a wiki account).
* Open a GitHub issue.
* Post your suggestion in the Freeskates Discord thread.

## Editing the Wiki Yourself

If you'd rather make the changes directly, you can:

* Submit a pull request on GitHub.
* Request editing permissions on the wiki.

Initially, editing permissions will be granted to active members of the Freeskates Discord community and to contributors who have consistently provided useful information.

## Before You Start Writing

Before creating or editing pages, it's recommended that you:

* Read the [Content Rules](/meta/editingrules) to understand the wiki's writing standards.
* Browse a few existing pages to get a feel for the formatting, style, and tone used throughout the wiki.

Don't worry about making everything perfect on your first contribution. Accurate information and a willingness to improve the wiki are far more important than flawless formatting.
