Due to the open-ended nature of the sport, there is a near-infinite variety of freeskate tricks. However, the modern progression layout is largely defined by the tutorial library on the JMK Ride YouTube channel (see the sidebar table for a foundational list).

Alongside these mainstream staples, underground creators like Uesan and various legacy channels have spent over a decade innovating entirely unique, independent trick families—such as the Bite Cycle Flip or Elevator 180° Down—that remain largely undiscovered by the wider modern scene.

For a structured roadmap on how these tricks rank and progress, check out the Kentei Class Progression system.
